About My Grandmother

My grandmother is 86 years old and has been a role model for everyone who knows her. She embodies resilience, wisdom, and strength, having lived through significant changes in society while upholding family values and traditions.

Early Life & Marriage

Born in Nepal, she was married at the early age of 9 years old, as it was customary during that time for parents to arrange early marriages to reduce financial burdens. Following her marriage, she walked for two days to reach her new home, where she began learning essential household responsibilities. Despite the challenges of adapting to a new life at such a young age, she embraced her duties with grace and determination.

Family & Legacy

Throughout her life, my grandmother has been the heart of our family, offering guidance, wisdom, and unconditional love. She has raised multiple generations with values of kindness, perseverance, and cultural pride. Her influence extends beyond our home, as she has always been someone people turn to for advice and support.

Strength & Resilience

Even at 86, she continues to inspire us with her unwavering spirit and wisdom. Her life story is a testament to the strength of women in Nepalese culture, where resilience and adaptability are crucial. She has faced hardships with a strong will, teaching us to approach life with patience and endurance.
